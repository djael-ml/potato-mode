# Privacy Policy - Potato Mode

**Potato Mode** is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed to be as "private by design" as possible.

## Data Collection
- **No Personal Data Collection**: Potato Mode does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
- **Local Storage**: All your settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using the browser's `chrome.storage.local` API. This data never leaves your computer.
- **No Third-Party Communication**: The extension does not communicate with any external servers, tracking services, or analytics providers.

## Permissions
- **storage**: Required to save your preferences (e.g., whether Dark Mode is active).
- **<all_urls>**: Required to apply the optimization styles and scripts to the websites you visit.

## Changes to This Policy
Any changes to this policy will be updated directly in the extension's repository.
